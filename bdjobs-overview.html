Case Study — Section 01 BDJobs · Mobile App Redesign

Overview

Reimagining Bangladesh's largest job platform with an AI‑driven, user‑centered experience.

About BDJobs

BDJobs is Bangladesh's largest online job portal, connecting millions of job seekers with employers across diverse industries. Since its launch in 2000, it has become the country's most trusted recruitment platform, offering job listings, resume management, employer services, and career resources through both web and mobile platforms.

Despite its strong market position, the current mobile experience presents several usability challenges — outdated navigation, limited personalization, unclear application tracking, and an interface that no longer meets modern user expectations.

Project Goal

Transform the BDJobs mobile experience into a modern, AI‑powered career platform — improving usability, simplifying critical user journeys, and introducing intelligent features such as personalized job recommendations, AI‑assisted resume creation, application tracking, and career guidance, all while preserving familiarity for existing users.

Design Challenge

The existing app enables users to search and apply for jobs but lacks a seamless, personalized experience. Users struggle with finding relevant opportunities, managing resumes, tracking applications, and understanding their progress — usability gaps that reduce confidence and add friction to the job search.

Design Vision

An intelligent recruitment platform that combines AI with human‑centered design — helping people discover better opportunities, build stronger resumes, receive meaningful career guidance, and confidently manage every stage of their job search.

A Glimpse Ahead

Familiar surface, intelligent core.

A personalized job feed with match scoring, and a clear application tracker that shows exactly where every candidacy stands — the two journeys at the heart of this redesign.
